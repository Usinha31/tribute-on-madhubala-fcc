Legendary actress Madhubala in various movies and phases of her life

A brief description of Madhubala's proffesional and personal life:

  • Madhubala was born on 14 February, 1933 in Delhi.
  • Her real name was Mumtaz Jehan Begum Dehlavi.
  • Her first screen appearance was in the movie 'Basant' (1942), at the age of 9.
  • Actress Devika Rani originally gave her the name 'Madhubala'.
  • She debuted as a lead heroine in the 1947 movie 'Neel Kamal' with actor Raj Kapoor.
  • She did more than 70 movies in her two decades career.
  • The 1960's classic 'Mughal-e-Azam' was the milestone of her career.
  • She was nominated in the best actress category of the Filmfare awards for Mughal-e-Azam.
  • She married actor and singer Kishor Kumar in 1960.
  • She was also known as the Marilyn Monroe of bollywood due to the similarities in looks, short
    span of career, and tragic end.
  • Her last movie 'Jwala' was shot in the 1950s, but was released in 1971.
  • She died from 'ventricular septal defect' on 23 February, 1969 in Mumbai.
